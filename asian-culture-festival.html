About the Festival

Celebrate. Connect.
Inspire.

Gateway to Asia is a free, family-friendly celebration of Asian traditions presented by the Wisconsin Chinese Chamber of Commerce. Bringing together culture, commerce, and community during Asian Pacific Islander Heritage Month, this festival invites all of Wisconsin to discover the beauty, diversity, and joy of Asian heritage — through live performance, authentic food, hands-on workshops, and cultural storytelling.

Explore the Cultures

Eight Traditions, One Festival

China

Lion Dance, traditional fan dance, classical instruments, Chinese calligraphy, lantern coloring, and dumpling folding.

🌿
Hmong Heritage

Traditional dance, the Qeej bamboo instrument, Pov Pob, Hmong textiles, and cultural storytelling.

India

Bollywood showcase, classical dance, henna art, spice discovery, and a family Bollywood mini dance class.

Japan

Japanese Taiko drums, karate, tea ceremony highlights, origami, and hands-on rhythm activities for families.

Philippines

Filipino folk dance, live music, Parol lantern craft, and family-friendly cultural participation.

Indonesia

Traditional Balinese and Javanese dance, gamelan music, and batik-inspired craft activities.

Laos

Traditional Lao dance and choreography celebrating the cultural heritage of the Lao community.

Thailand

Classical Thai dance, storytelling, and cultural craft activities rooted in Thai tradition.

Festival Zones

A Full Day of Discovery

🎭 Main Performance Stage

Scheduled cultural performances, martial arts demonstrations, live music, and community showcases running all day.

🍜 Taste Zone

Asian food vendors, culinary demos, bubble tea, sushi handrolls, and chai tastings.

🎨 Workshop Alley

Calligraphy, origami, lantern making, henna art, tea etiquette, Bollywood mini-class, and instrument try-outs.

👧 Kids Corner

Crafts, folktales, percussion play, and engaging cultural activities designed for children and families all day.

🤝 Community Marketplace

Cultural organizations, artisans, schools, community groups, and local businesses sharing their stories and products.

📸 Photo & Costume Station

Traditional dress-up and photo opportunities featuring kimono, sari, Hmong attire, and Baro't Saya.

Vendors & Participation

Join the Festival

We are welcoming food vendors, cultural organizations, artists, dance studios, and community groups to be part of Gateway to Asia. This is a unique opportunity to share your culture, products, and story with thousands of festival visitors.

🍱 Food Vendors

Asian restaurants, food trucks, and culinary businesses. Share authentic flavors with festival visitors in a high-visibility, high-traffic setting.

🎨 Marketplace Vendors

Artists, crafters, cultural artisans, and retail businesses. Showcase and sell handmade goods, cultural products, and unique items.

🏫 Community Organizations

Cultural nonprofits, schools, language programs, and community groups. Connect with the public and raise awareness for your mission.

🥋 Activity Partners

Martial arts studios, dance schools, and cultural educators interested in hosting interactive demonstrations or workshop stations.
